About

The Transportation Cost Intelligence Dashboard is an interactive geospatial analytics platform designed to monitor, analyse, and visualise Premium Motor Spirit (PMS) fuel prices and transport cost trends across all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory of Nigeria.

Data is sourced from the National Bureau of Statistics (NBS) monthly PMS Price Watch and Transport Cost Survey reports, covering 2024–2026. Fuel station locations are provided by OpenStreetMap contributors.
